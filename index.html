FAQs

Questions?
We've got you covered!

Will Participants receive activity points for attending Lumiere'25 ?

Activity Points are awarded in accordance with the regulations and norms established by KTU. The number of points granted varies depending on the nature of the event (Technical/Non-technical) in which the participant is involved.

Will all registered participants receive a Certificate of Participation for the event?

Certificates of Participation are exclusively granted to individuals who attend the event on the scheduled day. Participants who do not attend the event on the specified day are not eligible to receive the certification.

Will accommodation be provided for students attending Lumiere'25?

Accommodation will be available exclusively for students participating in the hackathon and the day-after events. Stay will be allotted to them only during this period.

What if a participant is unable to attend Lumiere'25? Will a refund be provided?

Unfortunately, we are unable to offer refunds if a participant is unable to attend the event. We encourage you to check your schedule before booking to ensure you can join us for this exciting experience.
